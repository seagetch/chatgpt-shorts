# Planning Template

Use this template when a managed short should keep its planning and revision axis stable in `idea.md`.

## idea.md

### Initial plan

- `① SF novelty`:
- `② present-day human theme`:
- `③ how ② changes in the world of ①`:
- `④ literary flavor`:
- intended ending direction:
- evaluation criteria:

Optional:

- setting:
- main characters:
- reveal or twist:
- ending image:

## Drafting check

Before writing, confirm:

- Is `①` visible in ordinary life, not just exposition?
- Is `②` recognizably about present humans?
- Is `③` the actual story engine?
- Does `④` affect scene choice and pacing?

## Revision loop

After the first draft:

1. Give the story a critical score out of 100.
2. List the main weaknesses behind that score.
3. Write a short revision policy.
4. Append that policy to `idea.md`.
5. Revise the story.
6. Repeat until the score is above 70.

## If the plan changes

Do not overwrite the original plan silently.

Append:

- what changed
- why it changed
- how the evaluation criteria changed
